Description

Mares Profundos is a private organization based on Ksma Heepi that specializes in underwater exploration and recovery operations. The organization is known for its expertise in deep sea diving and retrieval of lost or damaged equipment from the ocean floor. Mares Profundos maintains a close relationship with several corporations on the planet, who often contract the organization for underwater salvage operations.

Boss:

  • Name: Alejandra Navarro
  • Background: Navarro has been with Mares Profundos since its inception and has been instrumental in building the organization's reputation for excellence in underwater recovery operations. She is known for her tenacity and determination, often leading diving teams herself on the most challenging assignments.

Relations with corporations:

Mares Profundos maintains close relationships with several corporations on Ksma Heepi, including:

  • Sylph Industries: Mares Profundos often contracts with Sylph Industries Industries to conduct underwater recovery operations on behalf of the corporation. Sylph Industries Industries provides Mares Profundos with advanced underwater technology and equipment, while Mares Profundos provides Sylph Industries Industries with expert diving teams.

  • OrionTech: OrionTech contracts with Mares Profundos to conduct underwater research on behalf of the corporation. Mares Profundos provides OrionTech with access to the ocean floor and collects data on behalf of the corporation.

  • Spearhead Shipyards: Mares Profundos maintains a friendly but competitive relationship with Spearhead Shipyards. While both organizations compete for underwater recovery contracts, they often collaborate on larger operations that require the resources of both organizations.
